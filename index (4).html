Our Story

From Rift Valley
to Rocky Mountains

Rift to Rockies Provisions LLC was founded on a simple belief: the best ingredients deserve a direct path from farm to table. We partner directly with macadamia nut growers across East Africa's Great Rift Valley — one of the world's most fertile growing regions — to bring premium nuts to the Rocky Mountain market.

By working directly with our sourcing partners, we ensure fair compensation for farmers, uncompromising quality for our customers, and full traceability from harvest to your hands.

Based in Grand Junction, Colorado, we're proud to be the bridge between two remarkable landscapes.
